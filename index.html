My profile

Nancy is a Civil Engineer who is specialized in Geotechnical Engineering, she holds a bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST) and a master’s degree in Petroleum Geoscience from the University of Ghana. Nancy has always been passionate about giving back to society and building a generation of women in Tech. She lectured at the Civil engineering department of the Bolgatanga Polytechnic in the Upper East Region for almost 3 years where she managed to serve as her Department’s representative to academic board of Bolgatanga polytechnic. She also served as a union executive in her capacity as the general secretary and later as the vice-chairperson of the Bolgatanga chapter of the Polytechnic Teachers Association of Ghana. Based on the experience and lessons learnt she developed an interest in the area of Soil and rock related issues, and later left to pursue a Master’s degree in Petroleum Geoscience. She is also a member of the Ghana Institution of Engineers. In 2015, she started her own firm in Civil Engineering with particular focus in consulting, construction and soil laboratory services. Nancy’s firm has created job opportunities for 4 young people and tackling the issue of quality construction in Northern Ghana. She has been able to break barriers in her field of work and impacted on the lives of citizens through her consultations, education and projects implemented. She is a 2018 Mandela Washington Fellow under the business and entrepreneurship track at the university of Nevada- Reno. She has also been able to turn her love for baking into a business creating more opportunities for the youth.
